# Medical Services FAQ - Muscat International Airport

## Comprehensive Medical Services Information & Frequently Asked Questions

Based on official information from [Muscat International Airport Medical Services](https://www.muscatairport.co.om/en/content/medical-services)

---

### Q1: Where is the primary medical clinic located at Muscat International Airport?

**A:** The primary landside medical clinic is located in the main concourse in the ground floor level of the terminal building.

---

### Q2: Are medical treatments at the airport clinic free?

**A:** No, any conducted treatments will be charged.

---

### Q3: What types of medical services does the airport clinic provide?

**A:** The clinic will provide emergency medical services for patients in the terminal building, on the aircraft or during an airport emergency situation.

---

### Q4: What happens if a patient needs hospitalization?

**A:** Should the patient be assessed as requiring hospitalization, they will be transported from the airport clinic to a recommended clinic for the appropriate treatment.

---

### Q5: Where are the first aid rooms located in the terminal?

**A:** First aid rooms are located throughout the terminal building in North Pier departure and arrival levels and the departure level of the South Pier.

---

### Q6: What should departing passengers do if they need urgent medical attention?

**A:** Any departing passenger in need of urgent medical attention should contact the flight information desk.

---

### Q7: Is there a pharmacy available at the airport?

**A:** Yes, there is a pharmacy available in the departures level at the check-in hall.

---

### Q8: Which pharmacy operates at Muscat International Airport?

**A:** Muscat Pharmacy operates at Muscat International Airport. It is one of the oldest and largest pharmacies in Oman.

---

### Q9: How many pharmacy locations are there at the airport?

**A:** Muscat Pharmacy has two shops in Muscat International Airport, both airside and landside for the convenience of passengers.

---

### Q10: Where is the airside pharmacy located and what is the contact number?

**A:** The airside pharmacy is located near Gate B, beside PLENTY Restaurant. Contact number: +968 2435 6879.

---

### Q11: Where is the landside pharmacy located and what is the contact number?

**A:** The landside pharmacy is located near Oman Air Business Class Lounge Gate. Contact number: +968 2435 6878.

---

### Q12: What products does the airport pharmacy carry?

**A:** The pharmacy carries an extensive range of pharmaceutical, surgical, and consumer products, guaranteeing it is a one-stop shop for the medical and pharmaceutical needs of every traveler.

---

### Q13: Where can I get more information about Muscat Pharmacy?

**A:** For more information on Muscat Pharmacy, please visit: www.muscatpharmacy.net

---

### Q14: Are there medical services available for aircraft emergencies?

**A:** Yes, the clinic provides emergency medical services for patients on the aircraft during an airport emergency situation.

---

### Q15: Is there medical assistance available on both departure and arrival levels?

**A:** Yes, first aid rooms are located throughout the terminal building in North Pier departure and arrival levels and the departure level of the South Pier.

---

### Q16: Can I get medical assistance if I'm in the landside area of the airport?

**A:** Yes, the primary landside medical clinic is located in the main concourse in the ground floor level of the terminal building, and there is also a landside pharmacy near Oman Air Business Class Lounge Gate.

---

### Q17: Can I get medical assistance if I'm in the airside area of the airport?

**A:** Yes, there are first aid rooms in the departure levels and an airside pharmacy located near Gate B, beside PLENTY Restaurant.

---

### Q18: What is the contact information for medical emergencies at the airport?

**A:** For urgent medical attention, departing passengers should contact the flight information desk. For pharmacy services: Airside pharmacy +968 2435 6879, Landside pharmacy +968 2435 6878.

---

*This FAQ is based on official information from Muscat International Airport's medical services page. For the most current information, please visit https://www.muscatairport.co.om/en/content/medical-services*
