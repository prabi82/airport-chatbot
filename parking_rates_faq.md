Directions From Muscat: Take Sultan Qaboos Highway travelling west from Muscat City Centre, with the sea on your right-hand side. Muscat International Airport will be on the right side of the highway with a signpost. Take the exit signpost and Follow the slip road until you reach the flyover and stay in the right passing lane while following the signage. From Seeb: Take Sultan Qaboos Highway travelling east towards Muscat City Centre, with the sea on your left-hand side. Muscat International Airport is on the left side of the highway with a signpost. Follow the signage and take the slip road until you reach the flyover, then turn left, crossing over Sultan Qaboos Highway. Stay on the left passing lane before turning left to enter Muscat International Airport. From other parts of Oman: If arriving from other parts of Oman or going towards them, take Burj Al Sahwa roundabout, then go to the western side of Muscat International Airport that connects the capital city to routes across the rest of the country. Click here to view the map Pick Up & Drop-off Muscat Airport dedicated pick-up and drop-off areas in front of the departures and arrival terminals. For security reasons and in order to prevent congestion, the drop-off and pick-up areas are equipped with access systems to enhance passenger comfort and control traffic flow more effectively. Vehicles are not allowed to stay longer than 10 minutes in these areas; neither are drivers allowed to leave their vehicles unattended, or else they will be seized. Passengers who wish to stay longer than 10 minutes are recommended to use the short-term car parking. Where can I drop off passengers? Vehicles are permitted to wait outside the departure terminal building for free for a maximum of 10 minutes. Staying longer than 10 minutes in this area will incur a fee. For drivers who need more than 10 minutes, Muscat Airport recommends the short-term parking area. To ensure a smooth transfer to the check-in area, trolleys are available in front of the departure area in order to assist in carrying your luggage. Please note that for security reasons, and to ease congestion in the terminal building, only passengers with valid travel documents are permitted to enter the departures hall. For departing first and business class passengers travelling with Oman Air, a dedicated drop-off area, close to Oman Air’s First and Business Class check-in lounge, is available. Where can I pick up passengers? Vehicles are permitted to wait outside the arrivals hall for free for a maximum of 10 minutes. Staying longer than 10 minutes in this area will incur a fee. For drivers who need more time than 10 minutes, Muscat Airport recommends the short-term parking area. To ensure a pick-up without trouble, we advise families and friends coming to pick up passengers to check flight arrival status via Muscat International Airport website or application before departing for the airport. Car parking attendants are available on-site 24 hours a day, 7 days a week in case any assistance is required. Arriving in Oman Muscat International Airport warmly welcomes you with the same spirit of Omani hospitality. Once you arrive to the International arrivals hall, you can: Obtain a visa (if required). Exchange money. Clear immigration through the immigration desks or e-Gates. Go through customs clearance and declare any valuables. Enjoy a last minute shopping at the Duty Free shop. Collect your luggage from the baggage hall. Ask for help from a porter. Learn about Oman from the Ministry of Tourism Information Desk. Staff are available during peak periods to answer any queries you may have. If you are transiting through Muscat International Airport, follow the signs to continue your journey. Taxi service is available at Muscat Airport. Car Rental Upon arrival in Oman, car rental is an easily accessible option to benefit the maximum from your stay in the country. All offices are open 24 hours a day and are located in the arrivals public hall.
