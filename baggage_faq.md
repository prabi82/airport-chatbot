# Baggage FAQ - Muscat International Airport

## Comprehensive Baggage Information & Frequently Asked Questions

Based on official information from [Muscat International Airport Baggage Services](https://www.muscatairport.co.om/en/content/baggage)

---

### Q1: How many baggage reclaim carousels are available at Muscat International Airport?

**A:** Muscat International Airport has ten reclaim carousels at the arrivals hall, in which eight are International and two are Domestic. The Flight Information Display System (FIDS) indicates the flight number and the status of the luggage delivery for all inbound flights.

---

### Q2: Will my baggage be screened by customs at Muscat Airport?

**A:** Yes, all hold and hand baggage from International or Domestic flights may be subjected to screening by customs prior to leaving the baggage reclaim area. This applies to both international and domestic flights.

---

### Q3: What types of luggage can be handled at Muscat International Airport?

**A:** With the state of art system, Muscat International Airport can accommodate all types of luggage via automated handling at the check-in counters or using the out-of-gauge counters for luggage that do not comply with the allowed dimensions/weight.

---

### Q4: What are the baggage standards at Muscat International Airport?

**A:** As per Muscat International Airport standards, baggage must have at least one flat surface. Therefore, departing passengers must ensure that their baggage adheres to this standard. If not, the baggage has to be repacked.

---

### Q5: Are baggage trolleys available at Muscat Airport and where can I find them?

**A:** Yes, baggage trolleys are available for charge at convenient locations across Muscat International Airport. On departure, trolleys can be found outside the departures building and in all car parks. For arriving passengers, trolleys can be found in the baggage reclaim area.

---

### Q6: Are porter services available at Muscat Airport and what do they cost?

**A:** Yes, porters are available all over Muscat International Airport with prices starting from only 3.5 OR per trolley/porter. The service facilitates your travel experience by managing any or all of your luggage items within the airport. Porter service is stationed and available in both departure and arrival areas.

---

### Q7: What is Megaton Cargo Services and how can it help with excess baggage?

**A:** Megaton Cargo Services has 34 years of experience in the aviation industry and operates a kiosk at Muscat International Airport at the departure level. They offer a unique service of converting passenger's excess baggage to cargo for those passengers who are unable to pay higher excess baggage charges to airlines, with door delivery to their residence.

---

### Q8: What services does Megaton Cargo offer for excess baggage?

**A:** Megaton offers different services according to budgets: Express Courier Services, Economical Courier Services, and Cargo Services based on package urgency. Customers can choose delivery methods by Air, Sea, or Road. The deliveries vary on the number of transit days for receiving packages at the destination.

---

### Q9: Where is the Megaton Cargo kiosk located at Muscat Airport?

**A:** The Megaton Cargo kiosk is located at Office Location G1, next to Row G check-in counters, Departure Level 4, Muscat International Airport. Their contact details are WhatsApp: 00968-96585377 / 00968-79220113.

---

### Q10: Can I book Megaton Cargo services before my travel date?

**A:** Yes, customers can book cargo with Megaton Cargo Services prior to their scheduled date of travel, so packages can arrive as soon as they reach their destination. Excess baggage can be brought to the kiosk near the G check-in counter before checking in with the airline.

---

### Q11: What if I can't pay for Megaton Cargo services immediately?

**A:** If customers cannot pay transportation charges when handing over baggage, they can transfer payment to Megaton's bank account once they reach their destination. Megaton will accept and store the baggage at the airport until payment is received, then dispatch it to the residence address.

---

### Q12: What is baggage sealing and where can I get it?

**A:** Baggage sealing from Seal & Go protects bags from theft, damage, drug smuggling, opening, missing, and bad weather. Using innovative "shrink sealing" technology, a strong layer of transparent foil is heated and shrunk tightly around baggage in less than a minute.

---

### Q13: Where is the Seal & Go kiosk located at Muscat Airport?

**A:** The Seal & Go kiosk is situated in departure area C, next to the visa office. A second kiosk will be available at entrance B in the coming months. For more information, visit www.sealandgo.com.

---

### Q14: What is the Seal & Trace service?

**A:** Seal & Trace service allows you to trace lost baggage through registration on the seal and trace portal via a sticker attached to the exterior of the bag. Once registered online, the luggage is traceable in case of loss.

---

### Q15: What other travel products does Seal & Go offer?

**A:** Seal & Go offers a wide range of safety and travel comfort products including high-quality locks, stroller covers, backpack covers, and luggage straps to make travel as comfortable as possible.

---

### Q16: Can non-travelers use Megaton Cargo services?

**A:** Yes, customers who are not traveling but want to send items to loved ones back home can also use Megaton Cargo services for shipping their belongings.

---

### Q17: Can I have my cargo collected from my residence?

**A:** Yes, customers can request collection of their cargo from their residence and forward it to their destination prior to their scheduled date of travel through Megaton Cargo Services.
